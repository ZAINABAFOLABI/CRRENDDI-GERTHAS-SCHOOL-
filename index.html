SMART LEARNING PLAN

Well-rounded education is the best gift for every child. In this fast paced technology-age we find ourselves, it is imperative to design education curriculum, learning methodology, assessment and outcomes to meet up with today’s workforce and global demands while also creating a sustainable smart plan for the future.

OUR COMMITMENT

An average child without education progress challenges spends his first 24 years learning in a school with great optimism that this will lead him/her to having a “dream life”, it is simply fair that the institution called school live up to the responsibility to match up this child’s optimism with clearly mapped out strategies to achieve his life goals. CRRENDDI GERTHAS School accepts this responsibility and is dedicated to live up to it always.

MISSION

Our mission is to be the most sought after graduate students for employment, scholarships, distinguished fellowships and excellent novel contributions to knowledge and proactive problem solving.
